## What Are Messaging Credits?

Messaging credits are used when you send push notifications to wallet pass holders. Each notification sent to one fan uses one credit.

## How Credits Work

- **Free plan:** 500 credits/month included
- **Pro plan:** 5,000 credits/month included
- **Business plan:** 25,000 credits/month included
- **Additional credits:** Available in packs if you exceed your monthly allowance

### Example

If you have 1,000 wallet pass holders and send 3 notifications in a month:
- Total credits used: 3,000 (1,000 fans × 3 notifications)

## Optimizing Credit Usage

### Use Segmentation

Don't blast every notification to your entire audience:
- Promote local shows only to fans in that area
- Send genre-specific updates to interested fans
- Target high-engagement fans for premium content

### Batch Smartly

Instead of sending 5 separate updates in a week, combine them into one well-crafted notification.

### Monitor Usage

Check your credit usage in **Settings** → **Billing** → **Credit Usage**. You'll see:
- Credits used this month
- Credits remaining
- Usage trend over time

## Buying Additional Credits

If you exceed your monthly allowance:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Click **Buy Credits**
3. Choose a credit pack
4. Credits are added immediately and don't expire

---

*Credits reset monthly. Unused credits don't roll over.*
