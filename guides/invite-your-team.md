## Why Invite Your Team?

Most creators don't work alone. Your manager, label, marketing team, or merch partner may need access to your Airdope workspace to help manage drops, update your portal, or review fan data.

## How to Invite Team Members

1. Go to your [Airdope dashboard](https://app.airdope.com)
2. Click **Settings** in the left sidebar
3. Select **Team Members**
4. Click **Invite Member**
5. Enter their email address
6. Choose their role (Admin or Member)
7. Click **Send Invite**

They'll receive an email invitation to join your workspace.

## Roles & Permissions

### Admin
- Full access to all features
- Can invite/remove team members
- Can manage billing and settings
- Can create and publish drops
- Can view and export fan data

### Member
- Can create and manage drops
- Can update the fan portal
- Can view fan data
- Cannot manage billing or team settings

## Best Practices

- **Limit admin access** — Only give admin to people who need billing/settings control
- **Use work emails** — Makes it easier to manage access if team members change
- **Review access regularly** — Remove members who no longer need access

---

*Team members can be managed anytime from Settings → Team Members.*
