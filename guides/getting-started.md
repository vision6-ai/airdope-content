## Welcome to Airdope

This guide will walk you through setting up your Airdope account and getting your first fan engagement tools live. By the end, you'll have a working fan portal, your first wallet pass, and a clear path to growing your audience.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Head to [app.airdope.com](https://app.airdope.com) and sign up with your email or Google account. The free plan includes everything you need to get started.

Once you're in, you'll be guided through a quick onboarding flow that asks about your use case (artist, venue, brand, etc.) to customize your experience.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Fan Portal

Your fan portal is your digital home base — think of it as a supercharged link-in-bio page.

### Customize Your Profile

1. Navigate to **Fan Portal** in the sidebar
2. Upload your profile image and banner
3. Add your bio and social links
4. Choose your custom URL (e.g., `airdope.com/yourname`)

### Add Your Links

Organize your links into sections:

- **On Sale** — Ticket links, presale pages, merch drops
- **Music** — Streaming links, new releases, playlists
- **Links** — Social profiles, website, press kit

## Step 3: Create Your First Wallet Pass

Wallet passes are the heart of Airdope's engagement platform. They live in your fans' Apple and Google Wallets and give you a direct communication channel.

### Design Your Pass

1. Go to **Wallet Pass** in the sidebar
2. Choose a template or start from scratch
3. Add your logo, colors, and branding
4. Set up your welcome message

### Share Your Pass

Once your pass is designed, you'll get a unique link that fans can use to add the pass to their wallet. Share this link:

- In your fan portal
- On social media
- At live events (QR codes work great!)
- In your email signature

## Step 4: Create Your First Drop

Drops create excitement and urgency. Let's create your first one.

1. Navigate to **Drops** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Drop**
3. Add your drop details (title, description, image)
4. Set the drop window (start and end time)
5. Add a call-to-action link
6. Publish!
