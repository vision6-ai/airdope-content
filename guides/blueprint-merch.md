## The Merch Drop Blueprint

Limited-edition merch drops create excitement and drive sales. Here's how to use Airdope to launch merch that sells.

## Before the Drop

### Build Anticipation

1. **Tease on social** — Share a blurred preview or close-up detail shot
2. **Send a wallet notification** — "Something new is coming this Friday 👀"
3. **Create a countdown drop** — Build anticipation with a "Coming Soon" page

### Set Up Your Drop

- **Title:** Keep it clear — "Limited Edition Summer Tee" or "Tour Merch Drop"
- **Cover image:** High-quality product photo on clean background
- **CTA:** "Shop Now" → link to your merch store
- **Duration:** 24-72 hours for limited editions

## Launch Day

1. **Publish the drop** at the announced time
2. **Push notification** — "The drop is LIVE! 🔥 Limited quantities"
3. **Update your fan portal** — Add to the On Sale section
4. **Social posts** — Direct everyone to your fan portal

## After the Drop

- Share "sold out" or "last chance" updates to create urgency
- Thank buyers with a wallet notification
- Tease the next drop to keep momentum

## Tips for Better Merch Drops

1. **Limit quantities** — Scarcity drives demand. Even if you can make more, limit the first run.
2. **Use great photos** — Lifestyle shots > flat lays. Show the merch being worn.
3. **Bundle with experiences** — "Buy the hoodie, get early access to the next show"
4. **Time it right** — Drop merch alongside music releases or tour announcements for maximum attention

---

*Merch drops work best when they feel exclusive and time-limited.*
