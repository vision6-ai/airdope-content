## Why Connect Instagram?

Instagram is where many creators interact with fans daily. By connecting it to Airdope, you can automatically turn DM conversations and comment interactions into wallet pass subscribers and drop participants.

## Setting Up the Integration

1. Go to **Settings** → **Integrations** in your dashboard
2. Click **Connect Instagram**
3. Authorize Airdope to access your Instagram Business or Creator account
4. Configure your automation rules

## What You Can Automate

### DM Auto-Replies

Set up keyword-triggered auto-replies in DMs:

- Fan sends "TICKETS" → Auto-reply with your ticket link
- Fan sends "NEW MUSIC" → Reply with presave/streaming link
- Fan sends "PASS" → Reply with wallet pass signup link

### Comment Triggers

When fans comment specific keywords on your posts:
- Automatically send them a DM with the relevant link
- Drive them to your fan portal or a specific drop

### Story Mention Responses

When fans mention you in their stories:
- Send an automatic thank-you DM
- Include a link to your latest drop or wallet pass

## Best Practices

1. **Keep auto-replies natural** — Write them like you'd actually talk
2. **Use clear keywords** — Tell fans what to DM in your posts and stories
3. **Don't over-automate** — Personal replies still matter
4. **Test your flows** — DM yourself the keywords to make sure they work
