## The Music Release Blueprint

Releasing new music? This blueprint shows you exactly how to use Airdope to build anticipation, drive first-day streams, and keep the momentum going.

## Timeline

### 2 Weeks Before Release

1. **Create a teaser drop** — Share a 15-second snippet or album art reveal
2. **Push to wallet pass holders** — "New music coming 👀" with the release date
3. **Update your fan portal** — Add a countdown or "Coming Soon" link

### 1 Week Before

1. **Launch a presave drop** — Link to your presave page with a 48-hour window
2. **Send a wallet notification** — "Presave now, be first to hear it"
3. **Share on social** — Direct everyone to your fan portal

### Release Day

1. **Create the main drop** — Link to your streaming pages (Spotify, Apple Music, etc.)
2. **Push notification** — "It's here! 🎶 Listen now"
3. **Update fan portal** — Move the release to the top of your Music section

### Week After Release

1. **Share a behind-the-scenes drop** — Studio footage, lyrics breakdown, or commentary
2. **Thank your fans** — Send a personal wallet notification thanking early listeners
3. **Share milestones** — "We hit 10K streams! Thank you 🙏"

## Key Settings

- **Drop duration:** 24-48 hours for presave, ongoing for the release itself
- **CTA button:** "Presave Now" → "Listen Now" → "Watch the Video"
- **Tags:** Use genre and mood tags for better discoverability

## Pro Tips

- Stack your drops — presave, release, music video, behind-the-scenes
- Use different cover art for each drop to keep things fresh
- Time your wallet notifications for when your audience is most active

---

*This blueprint works for singles, EPs, albums, and music videos.*
