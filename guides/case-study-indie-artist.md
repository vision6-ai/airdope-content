## The Challenge

An independent artist with 15K Instagram followers and a small but growing Spotify audience wanted to sell out their first headline show (300 capacity) and build a direct fan relationship beyond social media.

## The Approach

### Month 1: Foundation

- Set up Airdope fan portal as their link-in-bio
- Designed a wallet pass with their album artwork
- Added wallet pass link to every social bio and email signature
- Ran an Instagram Story campaign: "DM me PASS for exclusive updates"

**Result:** 800 wallet pass signups in 30 days

### Month 2: Engagement

- Sent bi-weekly notifications: behind-the-scenes content, unreleased snippets
- Created a merch drop (limited edition tee, 48-hour window)
- Used fan location data to identify their biggest markets

**Result:** 2,200 total wallet pass holders, merch drop sold 150 units

### Month 3: The Show

- Announced headline show via wallet pass notification (first!)
- Created a 24-hour presale drop for wallet pass holders only
- General sale announced 48 hours later
- Day-of: sent venue info and doors time via wallet pass

**Result:** Presale sold 180 tickets (60% of venue). General sale sold out remaining 120 in 2 hours.

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Wallet pass signups | 5,200 (over 3 months) |
| Notification open rate | 92% |
| Presale conversion | 8.2% of pass holders |
| Show capacity | Sold out (300/300) |
| Merch revenue | $4,500 |

## Takeaways

1. **Start with your existing audience** — Instagram followers converted at 15% to wallet passes
2. **Consistency matters** — Regular, valuable notifications kept engagement high
3. **Presale exclusivity works** — Wallet pass holders felt special getting first access
4. **Location data is gold** — Knowing where fans were helped choose the right city
