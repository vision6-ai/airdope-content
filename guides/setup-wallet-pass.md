## What Is a Wallet Pass?

A wallet pass is a digital card that lives in your fans' Apple Wallet or Google Wallet. Once added, you can send push notifications directly to their lock screen — no app download required.

This is Airdope's most powerful engagement channel, with **90%+ open rates**.

## Creating Your Wallet Pass

### Step 1: Open the Wallet Pass Designer

1. Go to your [Airdope dashboard](https://app.airdope.com)
2. Click **Wallet Pass** in the left sidebar
3. Click **Design Your Pass**

### Step 2: Add Your Branding

Upload your brand assets:

- **Icon** — Small square icon (any size, appears on notifications)
- **Logo** — Your main logo (minimum 660px wide)
- **Strip Image** — The main visual on the pass (minimum 1125px wide)

### Step 3: Customize Colors & Text

- Choose your background color
- Set text colors for contrast
- Add a welcome message that appears when fans first add the pass

### Step 4: Set Up Notifications

Configure what types of updates you want to send:
- New drop alerts
- Show reminders
- Exclusive content
- Custom messages

### Step 5: Get Your Distribution Link

Once your pass is designed, you'll get a unique link. Share this link anywhere:

- In your fan portal (it's added automatically)
- On social media bios
- In email signatures
- As a QR code at live events

## Tips for Wallet Passes

1. **Keep your strip image eye-catching** — This is the main visual fans see
2. **Don't over-notify** — 2-4 push notifications per month is the sweet spot
3. **Use QR codes at events** — Print them on posters, screens, or hand them out
4. **Update seasonally** — Refresh your pass design for new tours, albums, or seasons
