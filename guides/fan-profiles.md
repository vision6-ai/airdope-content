## Your Fans CRM

Every fan who interacts with your Airdope — adding a wallet pass, visiting your portal, or engaging with a drop — gets a profile in your CRM. This gives you a clear picture of your audience.

## What's in a Fan Profile

Each fan profile includes:

- **Contact info** — Email, phone number (if provided)
- **Location** — City and country (from IP geolocation)
- **Engagement history** — Drops viewed, links clicked, passes added
- **Source** — How they found you (social, QR code, direct link)
- **Wallet pass status** — Active, removed, or never added
- **Tags** — Custom labels you can apply for segmentation

## Viewing Your Fans

1. Go to **Fans CRM** in the left sidebar
2. Browse the full list or use search/filters
3. Click any fan to see their full profile

### Search & Filter

Find fans by:
- Name, email, or phone number
- Location (city, country)
- Engagement level (high, medium, low)
- Wallet pass status
- Custom tags

## Using Fan Data

### Segment Your Audience

Create segments based on shared characteristics:
- **Local fans** — Fans within 50 miles of a venue
- **Superfans** — High engagement score, multiple drop interactions
- **New fans** — Added wallet pass in the last 30 days
- **Dormant** — No activity in 60+ days

### Personalize Your Outreach

Use segments to send targeted messages:
- Promote a show only to fans in that city
- Give early access to your most engaged fans
- Re-engage dormant fans with a special offer

## Privacy & Data

- Airdope is GDPR and CCPA compliant
- Fans can remove their wallet pass at any time
- Fan data is never sold or shared with third parties
- You can export your fan data anytime
