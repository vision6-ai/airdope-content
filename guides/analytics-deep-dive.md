## Your Analytics Dashboard

Airdope provides comprehensive analytics to help you understand what's working, what's not, and where to focus your energy.

## Key Metrics

### Fan Growth

- **Total fans** — All fans in your CRM
- **Wallet pass holders** — Active wallet pass subscribers
- **Growth rate** — New fans per week/month
- **Churn rate** — Fans who removed their wallet pass
- **Net growth** — New fans minus churned fans

### Engagement

- **Notification open rate** — % of push notifications opened (benchmark: 85-95%)
- **Click-through rate** — % of notifications that led to a link click
- **Portal visits** — Daily/weekly unique visitors to your fan portal
- **Link clicks** — Which links fans click most

### Drop Performance

- **Views** — How many fans saw the drop
- **CTR** — Click-through rate on the CTA
- **Conversion** — If tracked, how many completed the desired action
- **Revenue** — Attributed revenue from the drop

## Reading Your Analytics

### Growth Tab

Shows fan acquisition over time. Look for:
- Spikes around content releases or events
- Correlation between social activity and signups
- Which sources drive the most quality fans

### Engagement Tab

Shows how fans interact with your content. Look for:
- Notification open rate trends (declining = messaging too much)
- Peak engagement days and times
- Which message types get the best response

### Drops Tab

Shows performance of each drop. Compare:
- Which drop types perform best (music, merch, events)
- Optimal drop duration
- Best posting times

## Actionable Insights

- **Open rate dropping?** — You're probably sending too often. Reduce to 2x/month.
- **High views, low CTR?** — Your CTA isn't compelling enough. Test different copy.
- **Growth stalled?** — Time for a new acquisition push (social campaign, live events).
- **High churn?** — Survey churned fans or review your notification content.

## Exporting Data

Go to **Analytics** → **Export** to download:
- Fan list as CSV
- Engagement metrics as CSV
- Drop performance summary
