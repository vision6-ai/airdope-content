## The VIP Blueprint

VIP experiences are the ultimate fan engagement tool. They create unforgettable moments and generate premium revenue. Here's how to run them through Airdope.

## Types of VIP Drops

- **Meet & Greet** — Pre-show or post-show face time with fans
- **Backstage Access** — A behind-the-scenes look at your world
- **Soundcheck Party** — Let fans watch your soundcheck
- **Exclusive Listening Party** — Preview unreleased music with a small group
- **Virtual Hangout** — Video call with top fans

## Setting Up a VIP Drop

### Create the Drop

- **Title:** Be specific — "VIP Meet & Greet: NYC Show"
- **Cover image:** A photo from a previous VIP experience
- **CTA:** "Get VIP Access"
- **Duration:** Limited — once spots are filled, it's done

### Price It Right

VIP experiences command premium pricing. Consider:
- Meet & Greet: $50-150
- Backstage: $75-200
- Soundcheck: $30-75
- Listening Party: $25-50

### Limit Availability

Keep groups small (10-25 people) to maintain the exclusive feel. Use your merch store or ticketing platform to handle payments.

## Promoting VIP Drops

1. **Wallet pass first** — Give your most engaged fans first access
2. **24-hour window** — Keep it tight to drive urgency
3. **Social proof** — Share photos from past VIP experiences
4. **Email follow-up** — Send details and instructions to buyers

## Day-Of Execution

- Send a wallet notification with time, location, and instructions
- Have a check-in process (use the wallet pass as the "ticket")
- Capture photos/video (with permission) for future promotion
