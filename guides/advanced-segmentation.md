## Beyond Basic Lists

Basic email lists treat every fan the same. Advanced segmentation lets you send the right message to the right fan at the right time — dramatically increasing engagement.

## Segmentation Dimensions

### Engagement Score

Airdope automatically calculates an engagement score for each fan based on:
- Wallet pass interactions (notifications opened)
- Drop participation (views, clicks)
- Portal visits
- Link clicks

**Segments:**
- **Superfans** (score 80+) — Your most engaged. Give them early access and VIP treatment.
- **Active** (score 40-79) — Regularly engaged. Standard communication cadence.
- **Casual** (score 10-39) — Occasional interaction. Re-engage with compelling offers.
- **Dormant** (score <10) — Haven't engaged recently. Win-back campaigns.

### Location

Use geographic data to:
- Promote shows only to fans in that city/region
- Time notifications for the fan's local time zone
- Customize messaging for different markets

### Behavior

Segment by specific actions:
- Fans who clicked a merch drop but didn't buy
- Fans who presaved but haven't streamed since
- Fans who added the wallet pass from a specific event

### Source

Where fans came from matters:
- Social media converts may respond to visual content
- QR code signups from live events are likely local superfans
- Website signups may be more interested in content

## Creating Smart Segments

1. Go to **Fans CRM** → **Segments**
2. Click **Create Segment**
3. Add conditions (AND/OR logic):
   - Engagement score > 50 AND location = "New York"
   - Source = "QR Code" OR source = "Live Event"
4. Name your segment and save

## Using Segments

- **Targeted notifications** — Send different messages to different segments
- **Personalized drops** — Create drops visible only to certain segments
- **A/B testing** — Test messaging with different segments
- **Analytics** — Compare engagement across segments
