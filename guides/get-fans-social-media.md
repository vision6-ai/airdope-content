## From Followers to Fans

Social media followers are rented — you're at the mercy of algorithms. Airdope fans are owned — you have a direct line to them. Here's how to convert followers into fans.

## Strategy 1: Link-in-Bio

Replace your current link-in-bio with your Airdope fan portal.

- It's a more engaging destination than a plain link list
- Fans can add your wallet pass directly from the portal
- Every visitor is a potential wallet pass subscriber

**Where to add it:**
- Instagram bio
- TikTok bio
- Twitter/X bio
- YouTube channel description

## Strategy 2: Call-to-Action in Content

Include clear CTAs in your social content:

- **Posts:** "Link in bio to get early access"
- **Stories:** Swipe-up/link sticker to your fan portal or a specific drop
- **Reels/TikTok:** "Comment PASS and I'll send you the link"
- **YouTube:** Pin your fan portal link in comments

## Strategy 3: QR Codes

Generate a QR code for your wallet pass and use it:

- As a social media post or story
- In video content (hold it up, put it on screen)
- As your profile picture temporarily (for launches)

## Strategy 4: Exclusive Content Gates

Create drops that require a wallet pass to access:

- "Add my wallet pass to unlock the unreleased track"
- "Wallet pass holders get the presale code"

This turns your social content into a funnel for wallet pass signups.

## Strategy 5: Cross-Promotion

Partner with other creators:
- Feature each other's wallet passes in your portals
- Co-create a drop that both audiences can access
- Shout out each other's fan portals

## Tracking What Works

Check your Airdope analytics to see:
- Which social platform drives the most portal visits
- Conversion rate from portal visit to wallet pass signup
- Which content types drive the most engagement
