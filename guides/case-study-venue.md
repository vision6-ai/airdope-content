## The Challenge

A mid-size music venue (1,000 capacity) relied heavily on promoter-driven ticket sales and social media ads to fill shows. They had no direct relationship with attendees and no way to re-engage people who'd been to a show.

## The Approach

### QR Codes at Every Show

- Placed QR codes at the entrance, bar, and merch table
- Each QR linked to the venue's Airdope wallet pass
- Staff encouraged signups: "Add our pass for early access to upcoming shows"

### Weekly Show Announcements

- Every Monday: Push notification with the week's upcoming shows
- Personalized by genre preference (tracked from past attendance)
- Each notification linked to the relevant drop for ticket purchase

### Presale Access

- Wallet pass holders got 24-hour presale access to high-demand shows
- Created urgency: "Only for pass holders — presale ends tomorrow"

### Post-Show Follow-Up

- Day after each show: "Thanks for coming! Here's what's next..."
- Included photos from the show and upcoming related events

## Results (6 Months)

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Wallet pass subscribers | 12,500 |
| Weekly notification open rate | 88% |
| Repeat attendance rate | +40% vs. previous year |
| Presale ticket revenue | 35% of total ticket sales |
| Marketing ad spend reduction | -60% |

## Key Takeaways

1. **QR codes at events are the #1 acquisition channel** for venues
2. **Genre-based personalization** dramatically increased click-through rates
3. **Presale access** drove urgency and rewarded loyal attendees
4. **Direct communication** reduced dependence on expensive social ads
