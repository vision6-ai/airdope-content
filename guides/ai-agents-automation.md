## Let AI Do the Work

Airdope's AI agents handle repetitive engagement tasks so you can focus on creating. They learn from your audience's behavior and optimize timing, messaging, and targeting automatically.

## Available Automations

### Welcome Flow

When a new fan adds your wallet pass:
1. Immediate: Welcome notification with a personal message
2. Day 2: Share your latest release or drop
3. Day 7: Invite them to explore your fan portal
4. Day 14: Highlight an upcoming event or drop

### Re-Engagement Flow

When a fan hasn't interacted in 30+ days:
1. "We miss you" notification with your latest content
2. If no response in 7 days: Share an exclusive offer
3. If still no response: Reduce notification frequency to avoid churn

### Show Reminder Flow

For fans near an upcoming show:
1. 7 days before: "Show coming up in your city!"
2. 1 day before: "Tomorrow! Here's everything you need to know"
3. Day of: Venue info, doors time, set time
4. Day after: "Thanks for coming! Here's what's next"

### Drop Notification Flow

When you publish a new drop:
1. AI determines the optimal send time for each fan
2. Sends personalized notifications based on fan interests
3. Follows up with fans who viewed but didn't click

## Setting Up Automations

1. Go to **AI Agents** in the left sidebar
2. Choose a flow template or create from scratch
3. Customize the messages and timing
4. Set audience targeting (all fans or specific segments)
5. Activate the flow

## Smart Send Time

Airdope's AI analyzes when each fan typically opens notifications and schedules sends accordingly. Instead of blasting everyone at 2pm, each fan receives the message at their personal peak engagement time.

## Monitoring

- View active flows and their performance in the AI Agents dashboard
- See open rates, click rates, and conversion for each step
- AI automatically pauses underperforming flows and suggests improvements
