## The Tour Blueprint

Whether you're announcing a full tour or a single show, this blueprint helps you maximize ticket sales and fan engagement at every stage.

## Phase 1: Announcement

### Create the Announcement Drop

- **Title:** "Tour Announced: [Tour Name]"
- **Cover image:** Tour poster or city list graphic
- **CTA:** "Get Tickets" → link to your ticketing page
- **Duration:** Keep this live until tickets go on sale

### Notify Your Fans

- Send a wallet pass notification: "Tour dates just dropped! 🎤"
- Update your fan portal with the tour dates in the On Sale section

## Phase 2: Ticket Sales

### Presale Drop

Create a time-limited presale drop for wallet pass holders:

- **Duration:** 24-48 hours before general sale
- **CTA:** "Get Presale Access"
- **Wallet notification:** "Your exclusive presale starts NOW"

This rewards your most engaged fans and creates urgency.

### General Sale

- Update the drop CTA to "Buy Tickets"
- Send a reminder notification on general sale day
- Share ticket links across all social channels → fan portal

## Phase 3: Show Day

### Day-Of Engagement

- Send a wallet notification with venue info, doors time, and set time
- Create a show-day drop with merch pre-order or setlist reveal
- Update the wallet pass with venue-specific info

### At the Venue

- Display QR codes for wallet pass signup at the merch table and entry
- Run a live drop during the show (e.g., exclusive download code)

## Phase 4: Post-Show

- Send a "Thank you" notification the morning after
- Share photos/video as a follow-up drop
- Promote the next show in the tour

## Key Metrics to Track

- Presale conversion rate
- Wallet pass signups per show
- Notification open rate on show days
- Post-show engagement

---

*This blueprint works for solo shows, festivals, residencies, and full tours.*
