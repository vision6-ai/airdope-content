## Your Fan Portal Is Your Home Base

Your fan portal is the first thing fans see when they visit your Airdope page. It's more than a link-in-bio — it's a fully customizable hub that showcases everything about you.

## Accessing Your Profile Settings

1. Log in to your [Airdope dashboard](https://app.airdope.com)
2. Click **Fan Portal** in the left sidebar
3. You'll see a live preview of your portal on the right

## Customizing Your Profile

### Profile Image & Banner

- **Profile Image** — Upload a square image (at least 400x400px). This appears as your avatar.
- **Banner Image** — Upload a wide image (recommended 1200x400px). This appears as the header of your portal.

### Bio & Details

- **Display Name** — Your artist/brand name as fans will see it
- **Bio** — A short description (max 160 characters recommended). Keep it punchy.
- **Custom URL** — Set your vanity URL (e.g., `airdope.com/yourname`)

### Social Links

Add links to your social profiles. These appear as icons below your bio:
- Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, YouTube, Spotify, Apple Music, and more

## Organizing Your Links

Your portal has three sections for organizing content:

### On Sale
Ticket links, presale pages, and merch drops. Items here get prominent placement with a call-to-action style.

### Music
Streaming links, new releases, playlists, and music videos. These display with music-specific formatting.

### Links
Everything else — your website, press kit, podcast, newsletter signup, etc.

## Tips for a Great Profile

1. **Keep your bio short** — Fans scan, they don't read paragraphs
2. **Update regularly** — A stale portal signals inactivity
3. **Use a high-quality banner** — It sets the visual tone for your entire page
4. **Prioritize your links** — Put the most important ones at the top of each section
5. **Enable banner visibility** — In settings, make sure `sectionVisibility.banner` is turned on

---

*Your portal is live at your custom URL as soon as you save changes.*
