## Direct Fan Messaging

With Airdope, you can send messages directly to fans' wallet passes — appearing on their lock screen with 90%+ open rates. No email spam folders, no algorithm filtering.

## Types of Messages

### Push Notifications
Short, timely messages that appear on the lock screen:
- New release alerts
- Show announcements
- Drop reminders
- Personal updates

### Pass Updates
Update the content on the wallet pass itself:
- Change the strip image for a new era
- Update the back-of-pass with upcoming dates
- Refresh branding for a new album cycle

## Sending a Message

1. Go to **Wallet Pass** → **Send Notification**
2. Write your message (keep it under 100 characters for best display)
3. Optionally add a link (fans tap the notification to open it)
4. Choose your audience: All fans, or a specific segment
5. Send immediately or schedule for later

## Best Practices

### Frequency
- **Sweet spot:** 2-4 notifications per month
- **Too many:** Fans will remove the pass
- **Too few:** Fans forget about you

### Timing
- Send during your audience's active hours (check your analytics)
- Avoid early morning or late night
- Tuesdays through Thursdays tend to perform best

### Content
- Lead with value — give fans a reason to care
- Be conversational, not corporate
- Use urgency sparingly — save it for real deadlines
- Include a clear action: "Listen now", "Get tickets", "Shop the drop"

### Segmentation
Target messages based on:
- Location (promote local shows)
- Engagement level (reward superfans)
- Signup source (customize for different audiences)
