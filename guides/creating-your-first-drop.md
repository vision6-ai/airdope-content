## What Is a Drop?

A drop is a time-limited release that creates urgency and excitement. Drops can be anything — a ticket presale, exclusive merch, a new single, behind-the-scenes content, or a meet-and-greet opportunity.

The key ingredients are:

- **Limited availability** — Either time-limited, quantity-limited, or both
- **Compelling content** — Something fans actually want
- **Clear action** — A button that takes fans exactly where they need to go

## Planning Your Drop

Before you create a drop in Airdope, take a moment to plan:

### Define Your Goal

What do you want to achieve?

- Drive ticket sales for an upcoming show
- Create buzz around a new release
- Reward loyal fans with exclusive access
- Grow your wallet pass subscriber count

### Choose Your Timing

Timing matters. Consider:

- **Day of week** — Tuesday-Thursday typically see higher engagement
- **Time of day** — Match your audience's active hours
- **Duration** — 24-48 hours creates good urgency without being too restrictive
- **Relation to events** — Drop 1-2 weeks before a show for presales

## Creating the Drop

### Step 1: Basic Details

Navigate to **Drops** in your Airdope dashboard and click **Create Drop**.

Fill in the essentials:
- **Title** — Keep it short and exciting (e.g., "Exclusive Presale: Summer Tour")
- **Description** — Tell fans what they're getting and why it matters
- **Cover Image** — Use a high-quality image (16:9 ratio works best)

### Step 2: Set the Window

Choose when your drop goes live and when it ends:

- **Start Date/Time** — When should fans be able to access this?
- **End Date/Time** — When does the window close?

Pro tip: Announce the drop 24-48 hours before it goes live to build anticipation.

### Step 3: Add Your Call-to-Action

Every drop needs a clear action:

- **Button Text** — "Get Tickets", "Shop Now", "Listen First", etc.
- **Destination URL** — Where should the button take fans?

### Step 4: Preview and Publish

Review your drop in the preview pane, then hit **Publish**. Your drop will go live at the scheduled start time.

## Promoting Your Drop

Creating the drop is just the beginning. Here's how to maximize its impact:

### Push to Wallet Pass Holders

If you have fans with wallet passes, send them a push notification when the drop goes live. This is your most powerful channel — 90%+ of fans will see it.

### Share on Social

Post about the drop across your social channels. Include:
- The drop URL from your fan portal
- The start time and duration
- A clear description of what fans get

### Update Your Fan Portal

Your drop will automatically appear in your fan portal's On Sale section. Make sure your portal is linked in all your social bios.

## Measuring Success

After your drop ends, check your analytics:

- **Views** — How many people saw the drop
- **Click-through rate** — How many clicked the CTA
- **Wallet pass signups** — New subscribers gained during the drop
- **Revenue** (if applicable) — Sales driven by the drop

Use these insights to optimize your next drop.

## Tips for Better Drops

1. **Start small** — Your first drop doesn't need to be huge. Test and learn.
2. **Be authentic** — Fans can tell when you're being genuine vs. manufacturing hype
3. **Follow up** — After the drop, thank fans who participated and preview what's coming next
4. **Build a cadence** — Regular drops (monthly or bi-weekly) train fans to pay attention
