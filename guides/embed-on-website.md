## Website Embeds

Already have a website? You can embed Airdope directly into it — no migration needed. Add wallet pass signup buttons, drop widgets, or your full fan portal.

## Wallet Pass Button

Add a "Add to Wallet" button to any page:

1. Go to **Wallet Pass** → **Share**
2. Copy the embed code
3. Paste it into your website's HTML

The button automatically detects whether the visitor is on iOS (Apple Wallet) or Android (Google Wallet) and shows the appropriate branding.

## Drop Widget

Embed a specific drop on your website:

1. Go to **Drops** → select your drop
2. Click **Share** → **Embed**
3. Copy the code and paste it on your site

The widget shows the drop's cover image, title, countdown timer, and CTA button.

## Full Portal Embed

Embed your entire fan portal as an iframe:

```html
<iframe
  src="https://airdope.com/yourname?embed=true"
  width="100%"
  height="600"
  frameborder="0"
></iframe>
```

## Tips

1. **Above the fold** — Place the wallet pass button where visitors see it without scrolling
2. **Match your design** — The embed code supports custom colors to match your site
3. **Add to multiple pages** — Put the wallet button on your homepage, about page, and tour page
4. **Track conversions** — Use UTM parameters to see which page drives the most signups
